Pastries Recipe

1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
2. Roll out puff pastry dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
3. Cut the dough into squares or desired shapes.
4. Place a filling of your choice (e.g., jam, chocolate, or custard) in the center of each square.
5. Fold the pastry over the filling to form triangles, rectangles, or other shapes. Seal the edges by pressing with a fork.
6. Brush the top with beaten egg for a golden finish.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
8. Let the pastries cool slightly before serving. Dust with powdered sugar if desired.
