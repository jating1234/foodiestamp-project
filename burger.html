Burger Recipe

1. In a large bowl, mix minced meat (chicken/vegetarian substitute) with chopped onions, breadcrumbs, egg, salt, pepper, and your favorite spices.
2. Form the mixture into evenly sized patties.
3. Heat oil in a skillet or grill and cook the patties on medium heat for 3-4 minutes per side or until cooked through.
4. Toast burger buns lightly in a pan or toaster.
5. Assemble the burger: spread mayonnaise or any sauce of choice on the bottom bun, place the cooked patty, add cheese, lettuce, tomato slices, pickles, and onions.
6. Top with the second bun and serve with fries or a side salad.
