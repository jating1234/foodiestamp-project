Classic Margherita Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil (for drizzling)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough:
    • In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, and olive oil. Gradually add the yeast mixture and knead into a smooth dough.
    • Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and let the dough rise for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.
  2. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 250°C (480°F) or the highest setting available. Place a pizza stone or baking tray inside to heat.
  3. Prepare the pizza base: Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into a circle about 12 inches in diameter.
  4. Assemble the pizza:
    • Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base.
    • Add slices of mozzarella cheese on top.
    • Place fresh basil leaves and drizzle a little olive oil.
  5. Bake: Carefully transfer the pizza onto the preheated stone or tray. Bake for 7-10 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Serve: Let the pizza cool for a minute, slice, and enjoy your Classic Margherita Pizza!
