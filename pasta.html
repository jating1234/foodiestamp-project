Classic Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (penne, spaghetti, or your choice)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream or milk
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta:
    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt and the pasta.
    • Cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the sauce:
    • In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
    • Add tomato puree and cook for 5 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
    • Stir in fresh cream or milk, chili flakes, Italian seasoning, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Combine pasta and sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until the pasta is evenly coated.
  4. Serve: Transfer to a serving plate, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
